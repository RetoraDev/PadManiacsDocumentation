[title: "Credits Class"] ← Back to index

Scrolling credits screen with background slideshow and music.

[heading: "Description"]

Credits displays scrolling credit text with song credits, special thanks, and developer information. Features a rotating background slideshow using song banners/backgrounds and plays random music from the song library.
