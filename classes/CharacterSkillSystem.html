[title: "CharacterSkillSystem Class"] ← Back to index

Manages skill activation, effects, and cooldowns during gameplay.

[heading: "Description"]

CharacterSkillSystem handles the activation and management of character skills during gameplay. It tracks active skill effects, cooldowns, and provides hooks for skill-based gameplay modifications.
